Simple Summary: Fuse Charades is a completely offline game that collects no personal data, stores no information, and requires no internet connection.

Information We Collect

None. Fuse Charades does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data from users.

How We Use Information

Since we don't collect any information, we don't use any information. The app functions entirely offline on your device.

Data Storage

Fuse Charades does not store any data on your device beyond temporary game statistics (like current score) that are automatically cleared when you close the app.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services, analytics, or external connections. It's completely self-contained.

Children's Privacy

Since no data is collected, Fuse Charades is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13.

Network Communication

Fuse Charades does not require or use internet connectivity. All game content is stored locally within the app.

Audio

The app uses your device's audio system to play sound effects and timer notifications. No audio is recorded or transmitted.

Device Permissions

The app only requires access to:

  • Audio: To play game sound effects and timer beeps
  • Orientation: To detect device rotation for optimal gameplay

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new policy here. Since the app doesn't collect data, changes will likely be minimal.
